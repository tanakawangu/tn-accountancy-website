2. Our Services

We provide accountancy, bookkeeping, and related financial support services to UK individuals and businesses. The specific scope of services to be provided will be agreed in an engagement letter or service agreement prior to commencing work.

The information on our website describes our general service areas. It does not constitute a commitment to provide any specific service and services are always subject to engagement terms being agreed and any required due diligence being completed.

3. Engagement Process

Before we commence work, we will:

  • Confirm the scope of services to be provided
  • Carry out any required identity verification in accordance with anti-money laundering obligations
  • Confirm our fees and payment terms
  • Issue an engagement letter for your acceptance

We will not commence work until these steps are completed and the engagement is formally agreed.

4. Client Responsibilities

You agree to:

  • Provide us with complete, accurate, and timely information, documents, and records as requested

The quality of our work depends significantly on the quality and completeness of the information you provide. We cannot be responsible for errors or omissions arising from inaccurate or incomplete information provided to us.

5. Our Obligations

We will:

  • Provide services with reasonable care and skill
  • Maintain appropriate confidentiality over information you provide to us
  • Inform you of relevant deadlines within the scope of our engagement
  • Comply with applicable professional and legal requirements

Our fees will be confirmed in the engagement letter. We reserve the right to charge for additional work requested outside the agreed scope. Payment terms will be set out in the engagement letter.

We reserve the right to cease work if invoices are not paid in accordance with agreed payment terms.

We will keep confidential all information you provide to us in connection with our services, except where:

  • We are required by law to disclose it (including HMRC and regulatory requirements)

As an accountancy service provider, we are subject to anti-money laundering (AML) legislation. We are required to carry out identity verification checks on clients and to report certain suspicious activity. We may be required to take certain actions without notifying you where required by law.
